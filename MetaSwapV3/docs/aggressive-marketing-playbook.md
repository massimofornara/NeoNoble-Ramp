# MetaSwapV3 Aggressive Compliant Marketing Playbook

This playbook is designed to push growth hard without spam, false promises or regulated-finance misrepresentation.

## Positioning

MetaSwapV3 is production infrastructure for controlled token markets: token factory, RFQ liquidity, internal ledger, wallet synchronization, custody orchestration, proof systems and fiat payout rails.

Avoid claims such as guaranteed returns, passive income, risk-free yield, instant riches or "gold mine" as a user promise.

## Priority Segments

1. Token issuers with budget and distribution.
2. Brokers and fintechs needing embedded token markets.
3. Market makers, OTC desks and liquidity providers.
4. Web3 builders with real communities.
5. Compliance and custody partners.

## 14-Day Blitz

Day 1-2:
- Publish landing page.
- Set up lead capture and referral tracking.
- Build 500-account issuer ABM list.

Day 3-5:
- Founder-led outreach to 150 issuers per day.
- Partner outreach to 40 market makers per day.
- Publish 3 technical posts: RFQ liquidity, proof-of-reserves, token launch lifecycle.

Day 6-10:
- Run issuer launch cohort webinar.
- Offer 10 limited launch assessments.
- Push case-study style demos using testnet/sandbox wording unless live rails are funded.

Day 11-14:
- Convert qualified issuers to paid diligence.
- Convert liquidity partners to integration calls.
- Route hot leads to compliance/KYB first, then commercial terms.

## Paid Acquisition Angles

- "Launch controlled token markets without relying on AMM-only liquidity."
- "RFQ, ledger, custody and proof infrastructure for token issuers."
- "Embedded token markets for fintech platforms."

## Compliance Guardrails

- Every ad must include risk and eligibility language.
- No mention of guaranteed profit.
- No user-facing APY/yield claims unless legally approved and backed by product facts.
- No mass scraping or unsolicited bulk messaging.
- Every lead capture must include consent.
- Geo-target only licensed or pre-launch business-development jurisdictions.

## Weekly Growth Scorecard

- New leads by segment.
- Sales-qualified leads.
- Issuer diligence calls booked.
- KYB pass rate.
- Liquidity partner integrations started.
- Expected monthly volume pipeline.
- Expected monthly recurring fees.
- Compliance rejects and reasons.
